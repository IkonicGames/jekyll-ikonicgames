Hello, I'm Erin Gunn.

I am the sole member of Ikonic Games. I manage everything from development and design to business and marketing. My goal is to build a sustainable business making HTML5 and crossplatform video games. Currently, I am focussing solely on the HTML5 side of the business.

I enjoy making games of all genres, with a focus on smaller games with catchy mechanics. Most of my games are playable on just about any device you can find that has a web browser.

On this site, I also keep a blog where I discuss my development process and tools and write tutorials on their use. I am in love with the idea of giving back to the game development community that has already given me so much.

If you would like to get in touch with me or follow my progress, my social media accounts are on the sidebar to the right, as well as my email list.
